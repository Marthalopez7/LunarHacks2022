Sustainable Consumption

What is it and how you can help:

What is Sustainability?

Sustainability is the process of keeping ecological balance through one's actions. Nowadays, sustainability has grown in popularity, through the internet and social media. However, it has not grown enough to make adequate change in the world, and more people should practice sustainability. Being sustainable and following sustainable options is not very difficult. Most people in today’s society had in some way or form practice sustainability. More details will be provided in later sections.

The Issue

The unstable patterns of sustainability throughout history have caused the deterioration of Earth’s ecosystem. Trends in product production and consumption have taken a toll on the ecosystem, causing it to deteriorate. Some of the causes for environmental deterioration are excessive food waste, hazardous waste, energy consumption, CO2 emissions, and electronic waste. A change needs to be made for the ecosystem to bounce back and renew itself. Earth is the number one recycler!
